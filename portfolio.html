About Me

My name is Daniel, I am 30 years old and happily married.

Currently, I have been working for two years at a software house for secure credit solutions as a technical support for developers.
Soon, I will be a full-stack programmer with a lot of desire to show my talent for troubleshooting and finding effective solutions in the technical field and in the digital world.
The future is going digital. Let me take part in your progress towards a more developed future!
